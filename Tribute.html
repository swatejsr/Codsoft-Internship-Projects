Sir Ratan Naval Tata
Jewel of India
"During the 21 years he led the Tata Group, revenues grew over 40 times, and profit over 50 times. He received India's highest civilian honors — the Padma Vibhushan (2008) and Padma Bhushan (2000)."

Here's a timeline of Ratan Tata's life:

  • 1998: Launched the Tata Indica, the first Indian passenger car.
  • 2000: Tata Tea acquires Tetley, making it the largest tea company in the UK.
  • 2001: Re-entered the insurance sector through Tata AIG.
  • 2002: Acquired VSNL, expanding into telecom infrastructure.
  • 2003: TCS crossed $1 billion in revenue.
  • 2004: Tata Motors listed on NYSE; acquired Daewoo's heavy vehicle unit.
  • 2007: Tata Steel acquired European giant Corus.
  • 2008: Acquired Jaguar and Land Rover from Ford.
  • 2012: Launched Tata Starbucks in a joint venture with Starbucks.
